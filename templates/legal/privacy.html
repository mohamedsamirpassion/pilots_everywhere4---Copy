Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Account registration information (email, company details)
  • Profile information (services, equipment, certifications)
  • Location data when shared voluntarily
  • Job posting and application data
  • Ratings and reviews

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our marketplace services
  • To connect trucking companies with pilot car operators
  • To send notifications about relevant job opportunities
  • To maintain platform security and prevent fraud
  • To gather feedback and improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We share limited information only:

  • Contact information is shared only after job confirmation
  • Profile information is visible to potential partners
  • Location data is shared only when voluntarily provided
  • We never sell personal information to third parties

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@pilotseverywhere.com
